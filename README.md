NASSAU CANDY DISTRIBUTION ANALYSIS DASHBOARD 🍬

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Nassau Candy Distribution Analysis is a business analytics project focused on analyzing sales, product performance, customer demand, and distribution patterns for a candy distribution business.
The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help understand:
- Product performance
- Revenue contribution
- Customer purchasing patterns
- Distribution efficiency
- Sales trends
- Business growth opportunities

The project uses data analytics techniques to identify key performance indicators (KPIs) and support data-driven decision-making.

---
BUSINESS PROBLEM

Candy distribution businesses handle large volumes of sales and product data but often face challenges such as:
- Identifying best-performing products
- Understanding sales contribution by category
- Monitoring revenue trends
- Optimizing inventory and distribution decisions
- Identifying high-value customers and markets

Without proper analytics, business decisions become reactive rather than strategic.

This project addresses these challenges through structured data analysis and visualization.
